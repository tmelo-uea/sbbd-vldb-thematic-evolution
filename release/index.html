Following the Wave or Riding Alone?
A Decade of Thematic Evolution at SBBD vs. VLDB (2016–2025)

Tiago de Melo (Universidade do Estado do Amazonas) — research data and artifacts

Accepted at SBBD 2026 · Vision Papers track

Overview

This page provides access to all datasets, topic-modeling outputs, and figures produced for the study. The paper presents the first longitudinal, full-text topic-modeling comparison between SBBD and VLDB over the period 2016–2025, complemented by a bibliometric characterisation of both venues.

Data were collected from the SBC Open Library (SOL), vldb.org, OpenAlex, and Semantic Scholar. Topic modeling was performed with BERTopic using multilingual sentence embeddings.

Datasets

Full-text corpus

corpus_sbbd.jsonl
Full-text corpus of all 489 SBBD papers (2016–2025), one JSON object per line with {year, filename, text}. Extracted from the openly available SBC Open Library (SOL). The VLDB full text is not redistributed (copyright); reconstruct it locally with the provided pipeline.
489 papers · ~2.1M words · open access

Corpus-level bibliometrics

bibliometrics_comparison.csv
Year-by-year comparison of SBBD and VLDB across 14 bibliometric indicators: paper count, pages, authors, citations, references, international collaboration, and open-access rate (2016–2025).
10 rows × 15 columns
↓ Download CSV
topic_matches.csv
Semantic alignment between all 31 SBBD topics and 35 VLDB topics. Each row contains the best-matching VLDB topic for each SBBD topic, cosine similarity score, and a boolean match flag (threshold 0.60).
31 rows × 4 columns
